FAQ
Common questions
Do I need to be technical to set this up?

Not at all. If you can copy and paste, you can set up NoFrontDesk. The whole process takes under 10 minutes. You just connect your Guesty credentials, add your property details, and print the QR code signs.

Does this work with Expedia bookings?

Yes, that's exactly why we built it. When Expedia stopped passing guest contact info to PMS systems, automated check-in messages stopped reaching guests. NoFrontDesk solves this by letting guests self-serve at the property using a QR code — no prior contact needed.

What does the guest experience look like?

The guest scans a QR code at your property door, sees a clean branded page, types their last name, and is instantly redirected to their Guesty check-in form. The entire process takes about 20 seconds. No app download, no account creation.

Does this replace my Guesty check-in form?

No. NoFrontDesk is the bridge that gets guests to your existing Guesty check-in form. It's the front door to the check-in process — especially for guests who never received your automated messages.

What if two guests have the same last name?

NoFrontDesk shows a disambiguation screen with check-in and check-out dates so the guest can select their correct reservation. Each reservation links to its own unique Guesty check-in form.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. On the Growth plan and above, you can use a custom domain like checkin.yourproperty.com. On the Starter plan, your check-in pages are hosted at yourproperty.nofrontdesk.com.

What PMS systems do you support?

We currently support Guesty. Support for Hostaway, Lodgify, and other PMS systems is on the roadmap. If you'd like to be notified when your PMS is supported, sign up for our waitlist.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time, and you won't be charged for the next billing cycle.
